Black Hawk Down (Ken Nolan, 2002)


ΛOWMOVIE point ="5.9"


Mohamed Farrah Aidid, whose men collected the aid that came after the United Nations provided food aid to Somalia in 1993, caused the death of tens of thousands of people. In order to put an end to this incident, it was decided that a specially trained team would capture Aidid's two important men in a building in the city center. According to the plan, they were taken to the city by helicopter. They will go there, 2 men will be caught in the building and they will be returned without a hot conflict. This mission, which was planned to be completed in 45 minutes, suddenly turns upside down when one of the helicopters is shot down. After this, thousands of Somalis with a small number of soldiers begin a war that lasts 18 hours.








Citizen Kane (Welles,1941)


ΛOWMOVIE point ="7.6"


Wealthy media mogul Charles Foster Kane dies in his private castle, Xanadu. As he dies, he whispers the last word, "Rosabud." Various life stories about Kane are published in all newspapers. A young journalist is assigned to investigate the meaning of Kane's last words. The journalist contacts Kane's relatives. We also witness Kane's past. This individual story also shows the sad consequences of perfectionism and authoritarian management (hard marketing).
