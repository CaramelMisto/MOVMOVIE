Pirates of the Caribbean: The Curse of the Black Pearl ( Ted Elliott,2003)


ΛOWMOVIE point ="8.6"


The pirate life of the slightly devious but lovable Captain Jack Sparrow (Johnny Depp) is turned upside down when his enemy, the cunning Captain Barbossa (Geoffrey Rush), steals his ship, the Black Pearl. As if this were not enough, Captain Barbossa attacks the town of Port Royal and kidnaps the mayor's beautiful daughter, Elizabeth (Keira Knightley). In order to save the girl and recapture the Black Pearl, Elizabeth's childhood friend Will (Orlando Bloom) and Jack join forces. What Will doesn't know is how a cursed treasure changed Barbossa's fate and condemned him and his crew to live as immortals forever.
