The Prestige (Nolan , 2006)


ΛOWMOVIE point ="8.8"


In this mysterious story full of unexpected twists and turns, two Victorian stage magicians engage in a powerful rivalry that escalates into an increasingly violent war and an insatiable thirst to uncover each other's professional secrets. When these two magnificent men pit boldness against passion, showmanship against science, and ambition against friendship, the results are dangerous, deadly and deceitful. It all begins in rapidly changing London at the turn of the century. At a time when magicians are considered celebrities and idols of the highest order, two young magicians begin charting their path to fame. The flamboyant, sophisticated Robert Angier (HUGH JACKMAN) is a consummate showman, while the rough-and-tumble and traditionalist Alfred Borden (CHRISTIAN BALE) is a creative genius incapable of demonstrating his magical ideas. They hit the road as friends and partners esteeming each other. But when their biggest trick goes wrong, a lifelong feud begins between them; Both intend to surpass and upset the other. Their extreme competition grows with every trick, with every show; Until it knows no boundaries, and even includes the new and incredible powers of electricity and the scientific genius of Nikola Tesla...
