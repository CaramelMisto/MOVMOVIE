Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind(Michel Gondry ,2004)


ΛOWMOVIE point ="7.8"


Joel Barish (Jim Carrey)'s ex-girlfriend Clementine (Kate Winslet) has all her memories of their two-year relationship erased from her mind with a mysterious medical intervention. Learning this, Joel becomes very upset and decides to have the same procedure performed on himself. When he goes into deep sleep to erase all his memories, the days he lived with Clementine flash before his eyes. Joel realizes that he doesn't actually want to forget Clementine and tries to stop the intervention.








Ice Age ( Michael J. Wilson ,2002)


ΛOWMOVIE point ="8.5"


20 thousand years ago, the world was more wonderful and more dangerous than ever. There are few times when it is as dangerous as the beginning of the Ice Age. The planet's giant creatures and some of the smaller ones are migrating south to escape the cold. There are two people who disagree with these. Manfred, the wandering mammoth who likes to do things his own way, and Sid, the lazy man who loves to live without doing anything. The paths of these two cross and a wild tiger and a human baby they find are added to their adventures. Three very different animals team up to deliver the human baby to its family.
