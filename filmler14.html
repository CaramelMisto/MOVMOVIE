Black Swan ( Mark Heyman , 2010)


ΛOWMOVIE point ="7.9"


Nina (Portman) is a very talented ballerina living in New York and, like most ballerinas, there is nothing else in her life but dancing. She lives with her mother, Erica (Hershey), who is a former ballerina and is very ambitious in this regard. Play director Thomas Leroy (Cassel) decides to replace SWAN LAKE's prima ballerina Beth MacIntyre (Ryder) in the new season, and his first choice is Nina. Ballet needs someone who can portray the pure and elegant White Swan and the Black Swan, the representative of lust, at the same time. But there is a new rival waiting for Nina, and she has managed to impress Leroy. Although Nina fits the role of the White Swan, Lily is the exact counterpart of the Black Swan. As the rivalry between the two young dancers turns into a strange friendship, Nina begins to deal with her own dark side. The kind of indifference that could destroy him.
