Edgecomb has transported countless death row prisoners this way over the years. But none of them impress him as much as John Coffey. Coffey, a very large man, was sentenced to death for the murder of two little girls. Contrary to his spooky appearance, Coffey has a very emotional and complex inner world and has some supernatural powers. As Edgecomb gets closer to her, he begins to believe that miracles can happen in unexpected places.
